About us

The Cookie Jar is a cozy, charming bakery that captures the warmth and nostalgia of home-baked treats. Nestled in the heart of town, it’s the kind of place that instantly feels like a welcoming hug the moment you walk through the door. The air is always filled with the irresistible scent of freshly baked cookies, cakes, and pastries, all made with love and the finest ingredients.

The bakery’s signature is its wide range of cookies, from classic chocolate chip to more adventurous creations like salted caramel pretzel and lemon lavender. Each cookie is carefully crafted, with the perfect balance of crisp edges and chewy centers. Beyond cookies, The Cookie Jar offers a selection of decadent cakes and pastries, all made from scratch daily.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick sweet treat or to enjoy a leisurely coffee with a pastry, The Cookie Jar is the perfect place for anyone who loves the comfort of a homemade dessert. Friendly staff greet you with a smile, and the quaint decor, featuring vintage cookie jars and retro kitchen items, adds to the cozy atmosphere.

Whether it’s a pastry, a box of assorted cookies, or a warm chocolate chip cookie straight from the oven, The Cookie Jar is a place where every bite feels like a small moment of happiness.
